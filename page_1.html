Counseling and Psychological Services(CAPS)

At the University of Michigan, CAPS are committed to creating an environment based on our values of multicultural, multi-disciplinary and multi-theoretical practices that allow our diverse student body to access care, receive high quality services and take positive pathways to mental health.

CAPS provide a wide range of different services that provide differential mental health support.

Crisis Services

For students who need urgent support, CAPS offers crisis services to provide immediate help.

  • Urgent Support
  • After Hours Urgent Support
  • Community/National Resources
